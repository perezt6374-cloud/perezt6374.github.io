Barry Allen can run faster than light, travel through time, throw lightning, and even become one with the Speed Force...

Here’s a concise summary of Barry Allen’s (The Flash’s) powers:

  • Super Speed – Runs faster than light, moves across worlds, and channels the Speed Force without harm.
  • Enhanced Strength – Uses momentum from speed to deliver blows powerful enough to rival Superman.
  • Time & Dimensional Travel – Runs into the Speed Force to move through time or across universes, though often with major consequences (e.g., Flashpoint).
  • Time Remnants – Creates temporary duplicates of himself by interacting with alternate timelines.
  • Electrokinesis – Generates and throws Speed Force lightning, even creating constructs.
  • Speed Thinking – Processes thoughts, knowledge, and strategies almost instantly.
  • Speed Stealing/Lending – Absorbs or grants speed to others.
  • Becoming the Speed Force – At his peak, Barry can embody the Speed Force itself, the source of his powers.
  • Detective Skills – A brilliant forensic scientist and investigator, enhanced by speed thinking.

⚡ Overall: Barry isn’t just fast — his connection to the Speed Force makes him a reality-shaping hero, capable of changing time, space, and even universes.
